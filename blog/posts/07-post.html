The Tan Clan Generation Poem (譚係詩)

譚氏子孫班派
---------------------
英勇祖直前，惠福世代志。
撼天綻明光，照懷家情德。
繁華鏡震智，鳳宗承永流。

Pinyin Romanization
---------------------
Yīng yǒng zǔ zhí qián, huì fú shì dài zhì.
Hàn tiān zhàn míng guāng, zhào huái jiā qíng dé.
Fán huá jìng zhèn zhì, fèng zōng chéng yǒng liú.

English Translation
---------------------
Our brave noble ancestors led the way and gave us their legacy, aspiring to bring blessings and prosperity to future generations.
Our excellence shakes the heavens and radiates brilliant light, illuminating the sentiments, love, and moral virtues of our family.
With bountiful splendor that reveals truth and unleashes wisdom, our phoenix lineage shall endure and thrive for all eternity.
